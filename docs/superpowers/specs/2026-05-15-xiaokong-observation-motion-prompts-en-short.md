# Xiaokong Short English Motion Prompts

> Format: English action names, short English prompts.  
> Root motion is allowed for movement clips. Loop clips must start/end cleanly.

## Common Style

```text
Anime-style teenage girl in a short-sleeved school uniform dress. Natural modest movement, stable posture, small purposeful hand gestures for observing, checking, touching, pointing, or handling objects.
```
